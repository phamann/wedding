St. Helier

Raddisson Blu Waterfront

Situated on the outskirts of the town centre in St Helier. The Radisson Blu waterfront hotel offers rooms with views of St. Aubin's Bay, Elizabeth Castle, Elizabeth Marina or St. Helier town.

Enjoy the scenery at the newly refurbished waterfront restaurant and terrace and sip a cocktail as you look out over the marina at the waterfront bar.

www.radissonblu.com

Grand Jersey Hotel

The Grand Jersey Hotel & Spa, overlooking the captivating St Aubin’s Bay in St Helier. With a great area out side to enjoy a few drinks and scenic views, or have some time for yourself in their beautiful spa or even indulge yourself at the Michelin star Tassili restaurant which has been awarded a michelin star and earned three AA Rosettes in 2009

www.grandjersey.com

Both Hotels just a short walk from the town centre. Where you can find lovely local restaurants and shops.

St. Brelades

The Windmills Hotel

The Windmills Hotel, nestled above the locally famous St Brelade’s Bay where tranquillity and natural beauty create an idyllic setting. This family ran hotel is more than happy to help you enjoy your stay by sharing with you their love for the island.

www.windmillshotel.com

Seawold Guest House

The Seawold is ideally situated on the Island’s south coast, between the capital, St Helier, and St Aubin’s with the safe sandy beach of St Aubin’s Bay just across the road, where you can enjoy a wide range of water sports or simply relax.The Seawold is on the main bus route for all parts of the island and provides easy access to evening entertainment, which is to be had in the clubs and pubs of St Helier or a 15 minute walk to the more relaxed bars and bistro's in the picturesque village of St Aubin.

www.seawoldguesthouse.co.uk

Daisy Cottage Campsite

The only campsite in the west of the island, and are nestled in the heart of rural St Ouen, close to the beautiful and famous St Ouens Bay and the picturesque Greve de Lecq, The location and surrounding area lends itself to a relaxed atmosphere and boasts beautiful views of the local countryside, the coast of France and our stunning sister islands of Sark and Guernsey.

Daisy Cottage offers you the Choice of Camping in comfort in one of our Bell tents or new Serenity Tents and Shepherds Hut. Or you May pitch your own Tent, Motor home or Caravan.

www.daistcottagecampsite.com
