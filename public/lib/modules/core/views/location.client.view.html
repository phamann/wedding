Corbiere Phare

We are hosting our wedding in Gemma’s beautiful home of Jersey in the channel islands. The ceremony and reception will be at Corbiere Phare situated on the West coast of the island, over looking one of our favourite beaches.

Only 45 miles square, Jersey is 14 miles from France and 100 miles from the South Coast of England. A wealth of green lanes and beautiful beaches await you, with the freshest local seafood on offer. Once here - we think you’ll find it hard to leave!

Across from Corbiere Lighthouse, Corbiere Phare restaurant offers spectacular sea views and a magnificent sunset, the perfect place to celebrate our big day.

Getting here

For people whom don’t want to drive on the day, we have arranged a private bus to-and-from the venue leaving from St.Helier Liberation bus station.

The bus will be leaving promptly at 12:45pm arriving at the venue for 1:30pm (allowing time for traffic). This will be returning to the station after the days events at midnight.

Alternatively, guests are advised to organise their own taxis to take them to and from the wedding venue. These should take roughly 30 minutes form anywhere on the island. A list of local cab companies can be found below:
