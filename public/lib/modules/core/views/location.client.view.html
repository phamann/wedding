Corbiere Phare

We are hosting our wedding in Gemma’s beautiful home of Jersey in the channel islands. The ceremony and reception will be at Corbiere Phare situated on the West coast of the island, over looking one of our favourite beaches.

Only 45 miles square, Jersey is 14 miles from France and 100 miles from the South Coast of England. A wealth of green lanes and beautiful beaches await you, with the freshest local seafood on offer. Once here - we think you’ll find it hard to leave!

Across from Corbiere Lighthouse, Corbiere Phare restaurantx offers spectacular sea views and a magnificent sunset, the perfect place to celebrate our big day.
